Who is Charles Lima?

I enjoy experimenting with design, so I let the title cycle through a few of my favorite styles.

As of the creation of this page, I am a high school student finishing my third year in Pará, Brazil. I have been programming and drawing since my earliest memory and plan to dedicate my life to them until my last.

I have lived my entire life here, but my ambitions have always seemed to lie just beyond it. Do not misunderstand me, I am nothing without my homeland, as it is what made me. It was here that I discovered my passion for art, for the stars, and for numbers. I would be a lesser person without it.

However, as time passed, it became clear to me that I must eventually leave to pursue new opportunities and fully reach my potential.

This website was created as one of many steps toward that dream, something to show others, far from me, the works I’ve created and the love I’ve poured into them. Made in equal parts as an opportunity and a record, something I can look back on fondly once I enter college.

On the other pages, you will find a deeper analysis of my skills, learning, and process. This one simply explains my motivations.

Enjoy your stay.
