What are my achievements?

I enjoy experimenting with design, so I let the title cycle through a few of my favorite styles.

Astronomy and programming have always been passions of mine, subjects I’ve obsessed over for years. But it was in 2023 that I began putting my knowledge to academic use.

I received an opportunity to participate in national and international Olympiad exams through my former school, Ideal, in Pará. Through these competitions, I proved myself qualified and earned international awards and recognition for my knowledge of astronomy and programming.

I was awarded a gold medal in the National Astronomy Olympiad (OBA), being the only student across all Ideal units in Brazil to reach the international phase.

I also received an honorary mention for placing third in my school in the National Computing Olympiad (OBI), finishing just one spot away from advancing to the international phase and earning medals there as well.

These two achievements now define my drive, as they stand as proof that I am capable.
